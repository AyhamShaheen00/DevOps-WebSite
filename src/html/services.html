Streamlining Your Cloud Operations.

No matter what you're working on, who you're with, or how many of you there are, Celurean can help. These are our services.

Infrastructure Management.

This includes setting up, managing, and monitoring servers, whether they're on-premise or in the cloud. It also includes managing networking and ensuring that the infrastructure can scale and perform according to the needs of the application.

Configuration Management.

This involves setting up systems and software in a consistent and repeatable way. It could involve scripting system setup, deploying configuration changes, or using configuration management tools like Ansible, Puppet, or Chef.

Monitoring and Logging.

In order to ensure that systems are running correctly and to debug issues when they arise, IT operations teams set up monitoring and logging systems. This allows them to collect and analyze metrics and logs from their applications and infrastructure.

Incident Response.

When things go wrong, the operations team is often responsible for responding. This could involve diagnosing and fixing technical issues, or coordinating responses between different teams.

Security Management.

Operations teams are often involved in managing security, including setting up firewalls, managing access controls, applying security patches, and monitoring systems for potential security breaches.

Deployment and Release Management.

This involves managing the process of releasing new versions of software, which includes tasks like managing environments, scheduling deployments, and automating the release process to reduce errors and downtime.

Capacity Planning.

Operations teams are responsible for ensuring that there's sufficient capacity to meet the demands placed on the system. This includes planning for peaks in demand and scaling infrastructure up and down as required.
